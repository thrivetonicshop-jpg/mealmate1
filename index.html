Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Best Meal Mate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect pantry items, grocery lists, recipe preferences, and optionally your email if you create an account. Camera images are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. If you create an account, data syncs to secure cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services

If you enable AI scanning, images are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Account features use Supabase for authentication.

5. Your Rights

You can access, delete, or export your data at any time via Settings.

6. Contact

Questions? Email bestmealmate@gmail.com
